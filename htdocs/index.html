Welcome to ESGF CoG’s Administrator Guide¶

This Guide is intended for administrators of ESGF CoG sites. For anyone looking for help in using CoG to find or download ESGF data, please see https://esgf.github.io/esgf-user-support/user_guide.html.

    • Method 1: Enter your full OpenID
      • Step 1: Enter your full OpenID in the login box (Figure 1) and click “Login”.
      • Step 2: Enter your password in the ESGF OpenID password box
    • Method 2: Use the pull down to select your OpenID issuer.
      • Step 1: Use the arrow icon in Figure 1 to invoke the list of OpenID providers.
      • Step 2: Enter your username and password (Figure 3).
  • Forgot OpenID
    • Step 1: Go to the Login page
      • Step 2: Click the Forgot OpenID link located beneath the login box
    • Step 3: Fill out the template.
  • Forgot Password
    • Step 1: Click the Login link in the upper right corner of the CoG page.
    • Step 2: Click the “Forgot Password?” link on the login page
    • Step 3: Enter the ESGF OpenID and emal associated with that OpenID into the form and submit.

  • Getting Safari to accept the ESGF OpenID certificate
    • Step 1: Enter your ESGF OpenID (or select an OpenID provider from the pulldown) in the login box and click “Login”
    • Step 2: Click the “Show Certificate” button
    • Step 3: Expand the Trust section
    • Step 4: Change the Trust settings
    • Step 5: Authenticate
    • Step 6: Proceed with authentication
  • Getting Chrome to accept the ESGF OpenID certificate
    • Step 1: Insert your OpenID (or select an OpenID provider using the pulldown) in the login box and press “Login”
    • Step 2: Click “Advanced” on the warning page
    • Step 3: Click “Proceed to $node (unsafe)”
    • Step 4: Proceed with authentication.
  • Getting Internet Explorer to accept the ESGF OpenID certificate
    • Step 1: Enter your OpenID in the box and click “Login”
    • Step 2: Click the “Continue to this website (not recommended) link.
    • Step 3: Enter your password in the box provided
  • Getting Firefox to accept the ESGF OpenID certificate
    • Step 1: Enter your OpenID (or select your OpenID providing using the pulldown) in the login box and click “Login”
    • Step 2: Click on “Advanced”
    • Step 2: Click “Add Exception…”
    • Step 3: Click “Confirm Security Exception”
    • Step 4: Click “Resend”
    • Step 5: Proceed with authentication
